# Effective knowledge graphs are layered

## Context

Surfaced during reflection on how knowledge graph structure supports both human and agent consumption.

## Body

Effective knowledge graphs are layered. The layering principle means that no single consumer must parse the entire graph; instead, they navigate to the abstraction level that serves their current task.
